## FAQ Template

### 1. General Information
- *Q1: What is [Product/Service/Project]?*
  - A1: [Provide a brief and clear description]
- *Q2: Why was [Product/Service/Project] developed?*
  - A2: [Explain the purpose and motivation]
- *Q3: How can I stay updated on [Product/Service/Project] news and developments?*
  - A3: [Information on updates and channels]

### 2. Key Stakeholders
- *Q1: How does [Product/Service/Project] align with our company's overall strategy?*
  - A1: [Describe strategic alignment]
- *Q2: What are the expected outcomes and benefits for the company?*
  - A2: [Outline expected outcomes]
- *Q3: How can stakeholders contribute to or influence direction?*
  - A3: [Ways to participate]

### 3. Potential Customers
- *Q1: How does [Product/Service/Project] address my needs?*
  - A1: [Problem/solution fit explanation]
- *Q2: What makes it different from competing products/services?*
  - A2: [Differentiation and proof]
- *Q3: How can I trial or learn more?*
  - A3: [Trial/demo/resources]

### 4. Sales & Marketing
- *Q1: What are the key selling points?*
  - A1: [Main benefits and messaging]
- *Q2: Who is the target audience, and how should we approach them?*
  - A2: [Audience and channel approach]
- *Q3: Are there marketing materials or resources available?*
  - A3: [Available assets and access path]

### 5. Engineering
- *Q1: What were the major technical challenges, and how were they addressed?*
  - A1: [Challenges and decisions]
- *Q2: How does this incorporate the latest technology or innovation?*
  - A2: [Technical differentiation]
- *Q3: Is there ongoing technical support or development?*
  - A3: [Support and roadmap posture]

### 6. Product Owners
- *Q1: What are the long-term goals and roadmap?*
  - A1: [Roadmap and strategic intent]
- *Q2: How will feedback be collected and implemented?*
  - A2: [Feedback loop and governance]
- *Q3: How is success being measured?*
  - A3: [Metrics/KPIs]
